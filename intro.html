Introduction

Start here to learn about the past, present,
and future of self driving cars.

Since the beginning of the automobile, the dream of a car which can drive itself has always existed. Once thought to be a sci-fi dream, the reality of a self-driving car becomes more real every day. Of course, the self-driving car may be rooted in fiction, but current technology makes it closer to reality day by day.

Through key developments in the field of Data Science, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence, the integration between a system which can replicate human cognition and vehicles are helping develop the first self-driving cars. As data science is still a rapidly growing field, resources detailing how data science interacts with the automotive industry are not as abundant.

This site intends to serve as a learning resource about autonomous vehicles and the intersection between the autonomous vehicles and data science/machine. Organized below are sections covering the past, present, and future of Autonomous vehicles as well as a brief introduction to the topics of data science and machine learning.
